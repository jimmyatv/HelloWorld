HOW TO BOOK WITH US

Step 1

Submit an Inquiry Form

All clients must fill out an inquiry form before a call is scheduled. An OIA team member will then conduct a 30 min consultation via phone call to discuss your package, design, and production fee.

Step 2

Clients will submit a retainer fee based on the service selected.

Agreed fee terms are defined in a contract to be signed by all appropriate parties. If you are planning an event less than 7 days then there is an automatic $100.00 minimum rush fee.

Step 3

Now, the planning process begins!
